Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

⏱
6-minute increments
Log time in 0.1h increments — the standard unit for legal and professional billing. Fast entry, accurate records.
📊
Daily pace tracking
Know exactly where you stand against your targets every day. Adjusted daily targets account for holidays, leave, and remaining workdays.
📅
Monthly overview
A clean day-by-day breakdown of billable and non-billable hours, variance tracking, and cumulative progress for the month.
🌍
Statutory holidays
Automatic holiday calendars for all Canadian provinces and US states. Working day calculations are always accurate.
✈
Leave management
Add single days or date ranges for vacation and leave. Targets automatically adjust to reflect your actual availability.
📤
Export and import
Export all your entries to CSV for reporting or archiving. Import past entries from a spreadsheet to get started mid-year.
🔒

Privacy by design.

OneTenth tracks only hours and billing status — no client names, matter descriptions, or identifying information are ever recorded. Your billing data is anonymized by design.

No accounts. No servers. No data collection. Everything stays on your device, always.
